02 — Work

Built with three small ideas

01

scrollIntoView

Each nav link scrolls to its section with a native smooth behavior — no libraries.

02

Intersection Observer

Watches which section is currently visible and updates the active nav link automatically.

03

Progress bar

A thin bar at the top fills as you scroll down the page, in real time.

03 — Process

How the anchors work

  1. 1Each section gets a unique id.
  2. 2Nav links point to #id and are caught by a click handler.
  3. 3element.scrollIntoView({behavior: "smooth"}) handles the animation.
  4. 4An Observer watches scroll position and re-highlights the nav.
